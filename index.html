The sign up form for the Spring 2025 tree giveaway is live! Reserve your trees now.

After you reserve your shrubs and trees, you can pick them up on Saturday, March 29th between 12-4 pm at the Overlook Community Center, 611 W 12th St. Bloomington, Indiana.

The Neighborhood Planting Project is a collective of Bloomington residents building food autonomy.

Since 2017, we have given away over 27,000 trees, shrubs, and other native plants. In 2023, our biggest year, we gave away over 7,000 locally in Bloomington and another 5,000 through partner organizations around the state. This year, we're continuing the project with a lineup of native, edible fruit and nut trees, including pecan, persimmon, hazelnut, elderberry, black cherry, aronia- and more. We're also planning to have another seedling and garden supply swap on our tree distribution day in Bloomington.

To ask about opportunities to plug in, or any other questions or communications, please feel free to reach out to us at neighborhoodplantingproject@gmail.com. If you are a business looking to partner with us, shoot us an email!
